MBCL RETREAT IN SPAIN

Dec 1, 2019
Original post

"INITIATION RETREAT TO MBCL (Mindfulness-Based Compassionate Living) with Erik van den Brink...
He brought together a group of psychiatrists, psychologists, and MBSR instructors, people all interested in cultivating kindness and compassion as essential qualities to better relate to the difficult experiences we all experience at some point in our life. It has been a powerful, exciting and very transformative experience for those of us who have participated. Thanks to everyone for making it possible!!"

Sylvia Comas Barrera,
Mindfulness con Corazón, Barcelona

Many thanks, Sylvia. It was an honour to guide this MBCL retreat, supported by your heartful translations. Mindfulness con Corazón will organise more activities in Spain, including an MBCL Foundation Course and Teacher Training.
